Synopsis

When a mysterious signal arrives from beyond the Milky Way, a team of astronauts aboard the vessel Odyssey embarks on humanity's most ambitious mission. As they venture deeper into uncharted space, the crew discovers that the signal is not a message — it's a warning. Directed by Ava Chen, Nebula Rising is a breathtaking visual journey that combines hard science fiction with profound questions about consciousness and connection. The film has been praised for its grounded performances, stunning practical effects, and a third act that redefines the space exploration genre.
